Understanding Waste Types

Learn about the three main categories our AI can identify and how to handle each type

Organic Waste

Biodegradable materials that can decompose naturally and are perfect for composting.

Examples Include:
  • Food scraps (leftovers)
  • Dry waste
Color Code: Green

Anorganic Waste

Anorganic materials that can be processed and reused in manufacturing new products.

Examples Include:
  • Plastic
  • Glass bottles and jars
  • Metal
  • Paper
  • Cardboard boxes
  • Plastic bag
Color Code: Yellow

Non - Trash

Living organisms and natural elements that should not be disposed of as waste.

Examples Include:
  • Clothes
  • Shoes
  • Human
Color Code: Blue
